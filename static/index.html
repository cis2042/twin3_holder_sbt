📐
Calculation Method
How daily new users are counted from the blockchain
1
Identify mint transactions — Filter nft.transfers on BSC where from = 0x000...0000 (null address = mint event) and the sender matches the official minter wallet.
2
Group by UTC day — Truncate block_time AT TIME ZONE 'UTC' and count distinct to addresses per day.
3
Compute cumulative — Running sum of all daily counts produces the total holder curve.

📐
Wallet Quality Analysis Method
How we verify user authenticity
1
Identify today's new users — All wallets receiving their first SBT.
2
Look back 365 days — Count bnb.transactions where from = wallet in the prior year.
3
Calculate wallet age — age_days = mint_date - first_tx_date.
4
Classify into buckets — Age: <7d, 7–29d, 30–89d, 90–179d, 180d–1y. Activity: 0, 1–2, 3–9, 10–49, 50–199, 200+ transactions.
