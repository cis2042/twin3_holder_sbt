Daily New Users — 30-Day View

📐 Calculation Method & SQL
For each day in the lookback window, count distinct wallets that received a mint. 7-day moving average change = (avg of last 7 days − avg of prior 7 days) / avg of prior 7 days × 100%.
SELECT DATE_TRUNC('day', t.block_time) AS day,
  COUNT(DISTINCT t."to") AS new_users
FROM nft.transfers t
JOIN bnb.transactions tx ON tx.hash = t.tx_hash
WHERE t.blockchain = 'bnb'
  AND t.contract_address = FROM_HEX('e3ec...')
  AND t.block_time >= NOW() - INTERVAL '30' DAY
GROUP BY 1 ORDER BY 1
